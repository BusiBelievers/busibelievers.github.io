Partner With Busi Believers

Busi Believers Incorporated works with volunteers, churches, businesses, and community leaders to help individuals and families facing hardship.

Partnerships play an important role in the mission of Busi Believers Incorporated. By working together with local churches, businesses, contractors, and community volunteers, BUSI is able to provide practical assistance and mentorship to individuals facing hardship.

Ways To Partner

Project Sponsorship

Sponsor home repairs, vehicle repairs, or property maintenance projects for individuals in need.

Volunteer Support

Businesses and organizations may provide volunteer labor, equipment, or professional services.

Financial Sponsorship

Support mentorship programs, stewardship education, and community assistance efforts.

Community Partnerships

Churches, nonprofits, and civic organizations can collaborate with BUSI to strengthen communities together.
