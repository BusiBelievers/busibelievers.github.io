BUSI Curriculum Standards

This standard defines learning outcomes, completion rules, and facilitator delivery expectations for Program A and Program B.

Program A

Anger Management Track

Core Learning Objectives

  • Identify personal escalation triggers and de-escalation techniques.
  • Demonstrate accountability language during conflict reflection.
  • Apply structured communication in family, workplace, and community settings.
  • Complete restitution and responsibility action planning.

Completion Criteria

  • Minimum 90% attendance of required sessions.
  • All required worksheets submitted and reviewed.
  • Facilitator confirms behavioral plan is complete.
  • Pre/Post assessment completed.
Program B

Faith & Community Track

Core Learning Objectives

  • Build stewardship habits for time, finances, and relationships.
  • Strengthen faith-based decision-making and accountability.
  • Participate in community contribution and referral growth.
  • Develop a 30-day personal restoration plan.

Completion Criteria

  • Minimum 75% attendance of planned sessions.
  • Workbook and reflection activities completed.
  • Community action or referral milestone documented.
  • Pre/Post assessment completed.

Facilitator Delivery Standard

  • Every session must include objective review, instruction, guided practice, and reflection closeout.
  • Every session must produce a case note with progress and next-step commitments.
  • High-risk flags must be escalated to leadership within 24 hours.
  • All completion decisions require facilitator signature and date.
