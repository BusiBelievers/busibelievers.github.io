Mentorship & Leadership Programs in San Antonio

BUSI Program Pathway

The BUSI program is designed to help individuals rebuild stability through mentorship, stewardship education, and community support.

BUSI Applicant

Individuals requesting assistance begin by completing a participant intake form.

BUSI Participant

Participants sign the BUSI Stewardship Agreement and begin working toward personal growth and responsible stewardship.

BUSI Partner

Participants who complete the program become BUSI Partners committed to Building Unity, Stewardship, and Integrity.

BUSI Ambassador

Experienced participants may serve as mentors and help guide new participants.

BUSI Partners and Ambassadors help strengthen the community by Building Unity, Stewardship, and Integrity.

Community Impact & Accountability

Busi Believers Incorporated works to strengthen individuals and families through mentorship, stewardship education, and practical community support. Our mission is to restore hope, responsibility, and faith within the community.

Mentorship & Guidance

Providing mentorship and accountability to individuals facing financial hardship or life challenges.

Practical Assistance

Connecting individuals with support for home repairs, vehicle repairs, property maintenance, and other essential needs.

Stewardship Education

Teaching responsible decision-making, financial stewardship, and leadership development.

Community Partnerships

Working alongside volunteers, local churches, and community partners to serve those in need.
