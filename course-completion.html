Course Completion & Certificate Request

BUSI keeps the beginning of the curriculum easy to access, but certificates require verified participation. Complete the access check below before requesting certificate review.

Certificate requests are not automatic. BUSI must confirm attendance, assignments, assessments, payment or sliding-scale documentation, and facilitator approval.

Verify participant access

Enter the same approved BUSI reference and email used for curriculum access. The request form will open only after a verified participant record is found.

Certificate request is locked until participant access is verified.

Completion requirements

  • Participant intake and agreement completed.
  • Correct course track assigned by BUSI.
  • Required sessions or hours completed.
  • Workbook/reflection assignments reviewed.
  • Pre/post assessment or outcomes form completed when required.
  • Payment, sponsorship, or sliding-scale record documented.
  • Facilitator or BUSI representative approval recorded.
