Who We Are

Busi Believers Incorporated is a Christian service ministry dedicated to Bonding Using Stewardship and Integrity (BUSI). We serve primarily individuals in need by walking alongside them through practical help, mentorship, and Christ-centered support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to meet immediate life challenges while building long-term, Christ-centered relationships that restore faith, strengthen stewardship, and rebuild trust in God and in others.

What We Do

All services are provided without required fees.

  • Provide practical assistance through volunteer-led service
  • Offer stewardship education grounded in biblical principles
  • Build long-term mentoring and follow-up relationships
  • Help individuals establish goals, purpose, and stability
  • Model integrity, compassion, and accountability in service

Need Help?

If you are going through a difficult season and need guidance, practical assistance, or someone to walk alongside you, we’re here to help.

Our Faith

Our ministry is rooted in Christian faith and biblical values of stewardship, integrity, service, and love. While faith-based encouragement is woven throughout our work, all individuals are served with dignity, respect, and without coercion.
