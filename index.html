Organization Leadership

Busi Believers Incorporated is led by individuals committed to faith-based service, ethical leadership, and community support.

Founder & Director
Bernard Mitchell

Busi Believers Incorporated was founded by Bernard Mitchell to promote stewardship, integrity, mentorship, and community support. The organization exists to help individuals and small businesses overcome hardship through guidance, education, and faith-centered service.

How Your Support Helps

Donations to Busi Believers Incorporated directly support programs that provide mentorship, stewardship education, and practical community support for individuals and small businesses.

Mentorship Programs

Providing guidance and accountability for individuals working to rebuild their lives and strengthen their leadership.

Stewardship Education

Teaching responsible financial management and ethical decision-making for individuals and small businesses.

Community Assistance

Helping connect individuals with resources, support networks, and practical guidance during times of hardship.
