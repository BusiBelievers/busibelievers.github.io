What We Do

BUSI provides relationship-centered support that meets immediate needs while helping individuals establish goals and rebuild trust in God and others.

  • Practical assistance through volunteer-led service
  • Stewardship education grounded in biblical principles
  • Mentorship and follow-up relationships
  • Support navigating challenges and setting goals

All services are provided without required fees.

How BUSI Helps

BUSI is a faith-based ministry focused on relationship-centered support. We aim to help with immediate needs while also building long-term encouragement and stewardship.

  • Practical help through volunteers and community partners
  • Mentorship, accountability, and follow-up
  • Stewardship education and goal-setting
  • Prayer and encouragement

Emergency needs: If you are unsafe or in crisis, please contact local emergency services right away.
